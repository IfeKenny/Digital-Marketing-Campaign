This project analyzes the campaigns for 1000 campaigns across various marketing channels in which key metrics like Product Name, Category, Ad Spend (INR), Impressions, Clicks, Conversions, Revenue (INR), ROI, and Campaign Date were used to analyze the datasets

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of a Digital Marketing Campaign, highlighting key performance indicators (KPIs) and breakdowns by channel, product, and category.
Power BI FOR     visulaization and analytics, Excel as data source 
Core Performance Metrics
The campaign generated significant scale across the following high-level metrics:
Ad Spend: 2.42M
Revenue: 344.33M
Total ROI: 359.92K
Total Impressions: 242M
Total Clicks: 12M (yielding a 5.09% CTR)
Total Conversions: 1M

Channel and Category Breakdown
The data shows how resources were allocated and which areas yielded the best returns:

Ad Spend by Channel: Spending is relatively balanced across major channels, with Google Ads and Referrals receiving the highest investment, followed closely by Email, Instagram, and Influencer Marketing.

Top Categories: The Household category is the top performer, leading in both Conversion Rate and Sum of ROI.

Product Revenue: Shampoo is the highest revenue-generating product, followed by Wheat Flour, Soap, Tea, and Toothpaste.

Key Visual Insights
Engagement: The "Total Clicks vs. Total Impressions" donut chart shows that while impressions are vast (242M), clicks represent a 4.84% slice of that engagement volume.

Consistency: The line graph tracking Marketing Channels shows that Ad Spend, Conversions, ROI, and Clicks remain remarkably stable across all platforms, indicating a steady multi-channel strategy.
